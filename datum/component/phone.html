/datum/component/phone

Vars

calling_phoneA phone we are calling or has called us
do_not_disturbWhether or not the phone is receiving calls or not. Varies between on/off or forcibly on/off.
enabledWhether the phone is able to be called or not
holderWhat actually holds our phone, defaults to parent but may be set differently
last_callerThe Phone_ID of the last person to call this telephone.
networks_receiveNetworks that this phone can take calls from
networks_transmitNetworks that this phone can call
overlay_interactableIf the phone is activated by COMSIG_PHONE_BUTTON_USE or not. And if true Will only allow COMSIG_ATOM_BEFORE_HUMAN_ATTACK_HAND if recieving a call.
phone_categoryOur phone category which sorts us into tabs in the phone menu TGUI
phone_colorColor of phone displayed in the phone menu
phone_handsetOur connected handset
phone_iconOur phone icon that is displayed in the phone menu TGUI
phone_idThe id of our phone which shows up when we talk. Recommended to make unique. Do not include "(#:".
ringing_loopThe looping ringing sound when the phone is called
timeout_durationThe time it takes for our timer to end to cancel an attempted call and "go to voicemail"
timeout_timer_idThe ID of our timer to cancel an attempted call and "go to voicemail"

Procs

call_phoneStarts calling another phone, sets up connection between the phones

handle_reset_call_messageHandles any messaging we want when a call is reset
handle_speakHandles any messages that our handset 'hears' and passes to us. Passes the message to this phone and any connected phone via handle_hear()
holder_droppedHandles disabling the phone when the holder is dropped
holder_forcemovedTells our phone_handset that the holder has been forcemoved so the tether can be updated if need be
holder_picked_upHandles setting a specific phone ID and enabling the phone when the holder is picked up
item_used_on_phoneHandles any attackbys on our holder so we can hang up the phone
other_phone_picked_up_callHandles what we want to do when a phone we are calling picks up
override_deleteHandles bringing our handset back in case something tries to delete it
phone_availableWhether or not our phone is available to be called from other phones, returns TRUE if callable, otherwise FALSE. Note: this does not account for network compatability
phone_menu_usableWhether or not clicking on our phone will bring up the phone menu, returns TRUE if it will, otherwise FALSE
picked_up_callHandles what we want to do when we pick up a phone
post_call_phoneWhat we do after our call is set up from call_phone()
recall_handsetRecalls our handset back to holder and calls reset_call()
reset_callResets this phone's connection as well as anything we are connected to's connection, calls itself on any other connections
toggle_dndToggles do not disturb on or off, does not handle forced or unable do_not_disturb variables
use_phoneWhen we initially interact with the phone, whether opening the menu to call someone or picking up the phone being called
