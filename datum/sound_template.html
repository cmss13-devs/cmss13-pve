byond - Modules - Types — Var Details

/datum/sound_template

Vars

channelFor sound effects, set to 1 through 1024 to choose a specific sound channel. For values of 0 or less, any available channel will be chosen.
environmentChanges the environmental reverb for all 3D sounds until another environment is specified. The default value (-1) specifies no change in environment. A numeric value from 0 to 25 specifies a set of reverb presets for the environment.
falloffWithin the falloff distance a 3D sound stays at the constant loudest volume possible. Outside of this distance it attenuates at a rate determined by the falloff.
fileThis is the file that will be played when the sound is sent to a player.
frequencyAny value from -100 to 100 will play this sound at a multiple of its normal frequency. A value of 0 or 1 will play the sound at its normal frequency.
offsetCan be used to set a starting time, in seconds, for a sound.
pitchCan be used to shift the pitch of a sound up or down. This works similarly to frequency except that it doesn't impact playback speed.
rangeMaximum theoretical range (in tiles) of the sound, by default is equal to the volume.
repeatSet to TRUE to repeat the sound indefinitely once it begins playing, 2 to repeat it forwards and backwards.
statusAlter the way the sound is heard by affecting several different on/off values which combine as bit flags: SOUND_MUTE, SOUND_PAUSED, SOUND_STREAM, SOUND_UPDATE
volumeSet to a percentage from 0 to 100 of the sound's full volume.
volume_catThe category of this sound for client volume purposes: VOLUME_SFX (Sound effects), VOLUME_AMB (Ambience and Soundscapes) and VOLUME_ADM (Admin sounds and some other stuff)
waitSet to TRUE to wait for other sounds in this channel to finish before playing this one.
x_s_offsetHorizontal sound position offset.
y_s_offsetVertical sound position offset.
